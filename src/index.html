About Hacktoberfest

Hacktoberfest is an annual event across the world to encourage developers to contribute to open source projects.

Join us, give back to open source, meet fellow open source enthusiasts and enjoy a nice evening.

Whether it's your first step in open source or you already maintain a major project join us and let's hack for Hacktoberfest together.

Agenda

Welcome talk 18:30
Let's chat (or keep hacking!) over Pizza and drinks 20:30
Wrapup 21:30

During the evening Skyscanner engineers will help you get started and will be contributing along.

You only need to bring a laptop with you - drinks and food are on us!

Open source project maintainer? Prepare a lightning talk (~5min) to promote your project!

If you maintain any open source project feel free to join our event as a Maintainer.

Contact us at: BarcelonaTechEvents@skyscanner.net
